Info

There are two types of tests that should be executed on the candidate gateways. Namely ONE_WAY and TWO_WAY.

Basic One Way test verifies that a simple ONE_WAY push MEP scenario is successfully achieved from Corner 1 to Corner 2.

Basic Two Way testverifies that a simple TWO_WAY push push MEP scenario is successfully executed starting from Corner 1 to Corner 4 and all the way back to Corner 4 . 

Kerkovi is responsible (only for connectivity testing) to submit messages to backends in order to achieve the mentioned tests.
