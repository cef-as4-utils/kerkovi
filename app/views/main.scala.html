Kerkovi

The e-SENS generic AS4 Conformance & Interoperability bridge

Kerkovi consists of two national backend simulators and an as4 interceptor and aims to achieve connectivity between the AS4 compliant gateways and the testbed. Kerkovi is responsible of tracking the addresses of the gateways with respect to the party ID's and perform forwarding of messages with respect to the addresses it resolves from the party ID's.
