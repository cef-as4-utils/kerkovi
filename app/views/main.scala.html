Kerkovi

The e-SENS AS4 Conformance connectivity management tool

e-SENS AS4 conformance and interoperability testing environment consists of the national backend simulator, and the adapters that achieve connectivity between the AS4 compliant gateways and the testbed. Kerkovi is responsible of tracking the addresses of the gateways with respect to the party ID's and also performing the simple connectivity test (one_way, two_way)
