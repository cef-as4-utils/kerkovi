Dictonary meaning

Kerkovi is a Kurdish word that stands for Zebra .

It stems from two words joined: Ker + kovi.

Ker means donkey and kovi means wild.
Therefore, kerkovi means Wild donkey.

It does not have a common usage in the language. I have heard my parents use it in the Kurmanji dialect. Since there is no information about the word on the internet, I don't know whether it is also used in other dialects such as Zazaki and Sorani.

The word popularly used is 'Zebra', and too few people know this beautiful word. Now you are one of them :)
